Eminem's Awards

Since his debut in 1996, Eminem has 150 awards and 384 nominations so far.

  • Awards
    • Grammy Awards: Eminem has won 15 Grammy Awards, including Best Rap Album for "The Slim Shady LP," "The Marshall Mathers LP," and "Recovery," Best Rap Song for "Lose Yourself," and Best Rap/Sung Collaboration for "The Monster."
    • Academy Award: Eminem won an Academy Award for Best Original Song for "Lose Yourself," which was featured in the movie "8 Mile."
    • MTV Video Music Awards: Eminem has won 8 MTV Video Music Awards, including Best Video of the Year for "Without Me," Best Male Video for "The Real Slim Shady," and Best Hip-Hop Video for "Lose Yourself."
    • Billboard Music Awards: Eminem has won 11 Billboard Music Awards, including Top Artist, Top Male Artist, and Top Rap Artist.
    • BET Hip Hop Awards: Eminem has won 10 BET Hip Hop Awards, including Best Live Performer, Best Lyricist, and Best Hip Hop Video.
    • American Music Awards: Eminem has won 4 American Music Awards, including Favorite Rap/Hip-Hop Album for "The Eminem Show" and Favorite Rap/Hip-Hop Male Artist.
    • MTV Europe Music Awards: Eminem has won 3 MTV Europe Music Awards, including Best Hip Hop and Best Male.
    • People's Choice Awards: Eminem has won 2 People's Choice Awards, including Favorite Hip Hop Artist.
  • Nominations
    • Grammy Awards: Eminem has won 15 Grammy Awards and received 44 nominations, including Album of the Year for "The Marshall Mathers LP," "The Eminem Show," and "Recovery," Record of the Year for "Love the Way You Lie," and Best Rap Performance for "Rap God."
    • MTV Video Music Awards: Eminem has won 8 MTV Video Music Awards and received 29 nominations, including Video of the Year for "Stan," "Without Me," and "Love the Way You Lie," and Best Direction for "Not Afraid."
    • Billboard Music Awards: Eminem has won 11 Billboard Music Awards and received 49 nominations, including Top Billboard 200 Album for "Kamikaze," Top Hot 100 Song for "Love the Way You Lie," and Top Male Artist.
    • BET Hip Hop Awards: Eminem has won 10 BET Hip Hop Awards and received 46 nominations, including Best Hip Hop Video for "The Real Slim Shady," Best Live Performer, and Best Mixtape.
    • American Music Awards: Eminem has won 4 American Music Awards and received 21 nominations, including Artist of the Year, Favorite Rap/Hip-Hop Song for "The Real Slim Shady," and Favorite Pop/Rock Male Artist.
    • MTV Europe Music Awards: Eminem has won 3 MTV Europe Music Awards and received 17 nominations, including Best Male, Best Hip Hop, and Best Live Act.
